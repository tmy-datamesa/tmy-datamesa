# Hi there, I'm Tümay! 👋
### 🧠 Data Scientist | LLM Whisperer | Legal Tech Enthusiast

I'm the guy who went from a world of finance and engineering to teaching language models about Turkish law. My journey is about turning complex data into clear, actionable insights and convincing models not to hallucinate (with mixed success).

My motto: *"Prompting myself to be better everyday ✨"* — applies to both AIs and humans.

---

### 🚀 What I'm Up To

- 🤖 **Agentic RAG & LLMs:** Building smarter, more autonomous systems that can reason, not just retrieve. My goal is to make them ask the right questions.
- ⚖️ **Legal Tech:** Exploring how AI can make complex legal information accessible to everyone, not just lawyers.
- 📊 **Data-Driven Decisions:** Creating BI tools that translate operational metrics into financial impact, because numbers should tell a story that ends with a smart decision.
